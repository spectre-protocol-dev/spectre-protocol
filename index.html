// Protocol Flow

How It Works

Three steps to complete privacy. Powered by Groth16 zkSNARKs and Spectre commitment trees.

01

Deposit

Deposit SOL into the shielded pool. A cryptographic commitment is generated and anchored to the on-chain commitment tree. Your deposit becomes indistinguishable from all others.

02

Shield

Your funds rest in the privacy pool alongside all other deposits. A zero-knowledge proof is generated client-side, proving ownership without revealing which deposit belongs to you.

03

Withdraw

Withdraw to any fresh address via the relayer network. The zkSNARK proof is verified on-chain, confirming validity while maintaining complete sender-recipient unlinkability.

// Core Architecture

Built for the Void

Military-grade privacy infrastructure on the fastest L1.

zk

Groth16 Proofs

On-chain proof verification using BN254 elliptic curve. Proves note ownership and transaction validity without revealing any deposit details or addresses.

#

Spectre Commitment Tree

SNARK-optimized incremental hash tree with depth 20, supporting over 1 million deposits with a rolling 30-root history buffer for async withdrawals.

⇄

Relayer Network

WebSocket relay infrastructure handles withdrawal transactions on behalf of users, providing sender-recipient unlinkability through decoupled transaction submission.

∅

Nullifier Registry

On-chain nullifier tracking prevents double-spending while maintaining zero-knowledge. Each deposit can only be withdrawn once, without revealing deposit associations.

⚡

Solana Native

Sub-second finality. Deposits confirm in one block. Built natively on Solana with Anchor framework for maximum performance and minimal transaction costs.

🔒

Non-Custodial

Fully on-chain program controlled by PDAs. No trusted third party. No KYC. No logs. Your keys, your privacy, your SOL.
