// Protocol Flow

How It Works

Three steps. Complete privacy. Powered by Groth16 zkSNARKs and Poseidon Merkle trees.

01

Deposit

Deposit SOL into the shielded pool. A cryptographic commitment is generated and added to the on-chain Merkle tree. Your deposit becomes indistinguishable from all others.

02

Shield

Your funds sit in the privacy pool, mixed with all other deposits. A zero-knowledge proof is generated off-chain, proving ownership without revealing which deposit is yours.

03

Withdraw

Withdraw to any address via the relayer. The zkSNARK proof is verified on-chain, confirming validity while maintaining complete sender-recipient unlinkability.

// Core Features

Built for Shadows

Military-grade privacy infrastructure on the fastest chain.

zk

Groth16 zkSNARKs

On-chain proof verification using BN254 curve. Proves note ownership and transaction validity without revealing deposit details, amounts, or addresses.

#

Poseidon Merkle Tree

SNARK-friendly hash function with depth-20 incremental Merkle tree supporting up to 1M+ deposits with 33-root history buffer.

⇄

Relayer Network

WebSocket relayer service handles withdrawal transactions, providing sender-recipient unlinkability through decoupled transaction relay.

∅

Nullifier System

Prevents double-spending by ensuring each deposit can only be withdrawn once. Nullifier hashes are stored on-chain without revealing deposit links.

⚡

Solana Speed

Sub-second finality. Deposits confirm in one block. Withdrawals process through the relayer with minimal latency on the fastest L1.

🔒

Non-Custodial

Fully on-chain program. No trusted third party. No KYC. No logs. Only math. Your keys, your privacy, your SOL.
